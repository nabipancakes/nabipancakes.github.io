Memorial & Stratford Book Drive

March 24 - April 11, 2025 Memorial & Stratford Schools

Help us collect books for local schools and community centers. We're accepting gently used books for all ages, with a special focus on children's literature and young adult fiction.

Drop-off locations will be available at Memorial and Stratford schools during school hours, as well as at our main office on weekends.

Spring into Reading: 30 Day Book Challenge

April 1 - April 30, 2025 Virtual Event

Join our 30-day reading challenge to kickstart your spring reading habits. Each day features a new mini-challenge to help you explore different genres, authors, and reading practices.

Participants will receive daily prompts, book recommendations, and can join our virtual community to share their progress and discoveries.
