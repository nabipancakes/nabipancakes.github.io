Blind Date with a Book

At Bendwood Park, we organized a Blind Date with a Book event, where books were sold at a fraction of their original price to make them more accessible to all. Wrapped in mystery and curiosity, each book offered a chance to discover something new.

Our goal was to spread the love for reading and create opportunities for everyone, regardless of background, to experience the joy of a good story!

Painted Book Cover Auction

The Book Auction was a collaborative event with the National Art Honor Society at Memorial High School, where students painted original covers for a selection of donated books. These books will be auctioned to raise funds for local literacy initiatives and underfunded school libraries. This project aimed to promote access to literature while combining visual arts with community service.

All proceeds will go towards nonprofit organizations focused on education equity and/or literacy. Students received volunteer hours for painting, organizing, and running the event!

2025 Book Drive: Packed, Labeled, and Shipped

After months of collecting books, countless hours spent reaching out to local and national nonprofits, and the support of generous donors and volunteers, all books from our 2025 Book Drive have been packed, labeled, and shipped!

These boxes are headed to partners advancing literacy access across communities. Thank you to everyone who made this possible.
